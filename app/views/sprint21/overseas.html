You may be able to get Winter Fuel Payment depending on where you live.

If you live in Switzerland or a European Economic Area (EEA) country and you have a genuine and sufficient link to the UK, you may get a payment.

You can not get a payment if you live in Cyprus, France, Gibraltar, Greece, Malta, Portugal or Spain because the average winter temperature is higher than the warmest region of the UK.

Call 0800 731 0160 to make a overseas claim for Winter Fuel Payment.
